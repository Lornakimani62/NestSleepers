Services Provided

Our mission is to engineer customer-centric solutions that bring about safe, comfortable and economical trucks on the roads.

01.

Sleeper Installation

A truck sleeper cab is the section of a truck directly behind the seating area on the front cabin. The truck sleeper cabs are meant to be used as sleeping and resting areas by the driver.

Nest sleepers understand the importance of good rest for the drivers, which drives its desire to install comfortable and ergonomic sleeping and resting quarters in commercial trucks.

Having sleeping and resting quarters in a truck helps the organization and driver save some finances that would have been used to pay for motels and other resting areas.

02.

Diesel Heaters

Nest Sleepers installs, services, and repairs diesel heaters for commercial trucks. Diesel heaters are essential components for OTR drivers.

They are used to generate consistent heat to a truck’s interior and eliminate cold starting in engines. Elimination of cold starting adds up to the efficiency of a truck while having consistency in the cabin’s temperature results in a more comfortable experience.

Through installation, servicing, and repairing diesel heaters, Nest Sleepers is able to realize its mission of ensuring that trucks are comfortable and economical.

03.

Air Conditioning (AC) System Installation

Nest Sleepers has specialized in installing, servicing, and repairing AC systems of commercial trucks and cargo vans.

AC systems are essential elements in vehicles as they aid in achieving a comfortable, fun, and safer driving experience

Trailer Repair

The road cargo transportation industry in the US relies heavily on the use of trailers. Nest Sleepers offers customization and repair services for large and small trailers.

Customization significantly helps in building a brand identity for an organization in the transport industry, while repairs on trailers will ensure that the roads are safer

Cargo Van Customization

Nest Sleepers prides itself in delivering excellence in the customization of cargo vans. Cargo vans are crucial for transportation companies as they are best suited for minimal luggage over relatively short distances.

Customization of cargo vans brings about the creation of brand identity for organizations in the transport industry. It also brings about economic value as the cargo vans are designed to best suit the type of cargo transported.
